# Outreach Templates

One template per target industry, tuned to what makes that industry buy
partnerships. Style rules for every draft:

- **Under 120 words.** One personalized hook, one clear ask.
- Subject lines ≤ 7 words, no clickbait, company name or hook in it.
- The hook (`{{Hook}}`) must be REAL — pulled from ZoomInfo scoops/news or web
  research (a sponsorship they run, an award, an expansion, a new location).
  Never fake familiarity.
- CTA is always a 15-minute call, with a specific day offered.
- Merge fields: `{{FirstName}}`, `{{Company}}`, `{{City}}`, `{{Hook}}`, plus
  seller fields from `config/profile.md`.
- Pick the template by the prospect's industry; use `generic.md` when none fit.
